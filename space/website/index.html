About SpaceClean

Protecting Our Orbital Environment

SpaceClean is a hackathon project dedicated to solving one of space's biggest challenges: orbital debris management. With over 34,000 tracked objects orbiting Earth at speeds up to 28,000 km/h, even a tiny fragment can destroy a satellite.

We combine TensorFlow.js for real-time camera-based detection, Python/FastAPI for backend services, and Scikit-learn Random Forest models for collision probability prediction — all integrated into one unified platform.
